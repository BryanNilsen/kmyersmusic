ABOUT

Philadelphia-based singer-songwriter, Kaitlyn Myers, takes inspiration from the vintage sounds of Motown and soul, but adds the modern rhythms of today’s pop music. Her passion for mental health and social justice are vividly reflected in her lyrics.

In her debut EP, “Chasing Dopamine” (produced by Shannon McArthur), Kaitlyn discusses her trifles with depersonalization and anxiety throughout all five songs. In her more recent single, “Star Spangled Revolution”, she modified the American National Anthem to represent the Black Lives Matter Movement, donating all proceeds to organizations that support the cause (Learn More under the Activism tab).

Kaitlyn Myers is beyond grateful for the supportive following she has received on TikTok. She is currently writing more original music and looks forward to more releases this Spring! All in all, Kaitlyn Myers has huge plans for the future and she hopes to share her voice to anyone that will take the time to listen.
