GET INVOLVED

Activism is an important part of my life and my art. This tab is dedicated to this ongoing activism. Consider this my vow to use my platform to work towards justice, equality, and human rights. Here are a few of my projects so far:

“Star Spangled Revolution”
This single donates all proceeds to organizations that benefit the Black Lives Matter Movement. I am thrilled to announce that thanks to your help, we have raised over $1,000 and counting to the following organizations:

  • Black Lives Matter
  • Afrotectopia
  • Campaign Zero
  • The Bail Project

“Star Spangled Revolution” stickers with Miscellaneous Squid
I teamed up with Miscellaneous Squid, an online sticker shop dedicated to activism, and the talented Eleni Trapp to create this wonderful sticker. The cover design is by Eleni Trapp, and all profits from this sticker will go to organizations that support BLM.
